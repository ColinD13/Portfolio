Board Game Odds Calculator - React JS

Recently my friends and I have been playing a board game called Villianous. One of my friends mentioned that it would be helpful to know the odds of drawing each card in your deck, so I decided to take action and created this website. This website lets the user choose what deck they are playing and then lets them enter what cards they are drawing, and in response it shows the odds to draw every other card in the deck. This is my first project in React Js and I am very happy with how it has turned out so far. I also have some other ideas to keep adding to this project, but in the meantime this is on hold. I hosted this projecty through vercel and you can check it out here! -> Villianous Odds
